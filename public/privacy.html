Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

LiveMore ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

LiveMore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data (including streaks, XP, challenges, and settings) is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

No Accounts Required

LiveMore does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, cookies, or third-party services that collect data about you or your usage.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share with third parties.

Sharing Features

The app includes optional sharing features (share your streak, challenge a friend). These use your device's native share functionality. We do not see, store, or have access to any content you choose to share.

Children's Privacy

LiveMore does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@livemoreapp.com.
